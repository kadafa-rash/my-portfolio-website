About Me

Faith Jepngetich is a businesswoman and aspiring software developer, currently studying front-end web development at ALX Africa while building a personal brand focused on sustainable innovation and digital impact. Passionate about solving social and environmental challenges through tech, she has contributed to impactful projects—including the UI design of a mobile app tackling deforestation in the Congo and proposing automated irrigation and organic fertilizer solutions. With a goal to merge her entrepreneurial experience with technology, Faith aims to create scalable, community-driven solutions in agriculture and conservation.
